Frequently Asked Questions

What types of plants do you offer?

We offer a wide range of plants, including indoor plants, outdoor plants, flowering plants, medicinal plants, fruit plants, bonsai, succulents, shrubs, and trees. We also cater to bulk orders for landscaping and commercial projects.

Do you supply plants in bulk for businesses, landscapers, or developers?

Yes, we specialize in large-scale plant supply for commercial projects, landscapers, corporate offices, real estate developers, and government contracts. We can fulfill bulk orders with customized plant selections.

Can I visit your nursery and buy plants directly?

Absolutely! We welcome walk-in customers as well as bulk buyers. Our nursery is open throughout the week, and our expert team is always available to assist you in choosing the right plants.

Do you provide nationwide delivery for bulk orders?

Yes, we have a well-established logistics network to deliver plants safely across the country. Our plants are packaged carefully to ensure they arrive in perfect condition.

What are the best low-maintenance plants for homes and offices?

For homes and offices, we recommend snake plants, pothos, ZZ plants, rubber plants, peace lilies, and spider plants. These require minimal care and thrive in indoor environments.

Do you offer landscaping services?

Yes, we provide complete landscaping solutions, from design consultation to plant selection and installation. We work with residential projects, commercial properties, resorts, and public spaces.

How do I take care of my outdoor plants?

Outdoor plants need proper sunlight, well-draining soil, and regular watering. We provide customized care guides depending on the plant variety, climate, and season.

Can I get assistance in selecting plants for my garden?

Of course! Our plant experts can help you choose the best plants based on your location, soil type, climate, and personal preferences.

Do you offer organic fertilizers and plant care products?

Yes, we stock high-quality organic fertilizers, potting mixes, pest control solutions, and soil conditioners to keep your plants healthy.

What are the best plants for balconies or rooftop gardens?

For balconies and rooftops, we recommend hardy plants like bougainvillea, areca palm, jade plants, money plants, hibiscus, and dwarf fruit trees, which thrive in open environments.

Can you provide plants for events and corporate gifting?

Yes, we supply plants for weddings, corporate events, hotels, and gifting purposes. We also offer personalized pot branding for corporate gifts.

Do you have seasonal plants and flowers available?

Yes, we offer a variety of seasonal flowering plants, including marigolds, petunias, dahlias, and chrysanthemums, based on availability.

How can I prevent pests from damaging my plants?

Regular monitoring, proper watering, and using organic pesticides can help. We also offer natural pest control solutions that are safe for your plants.

Do you sell rare and exotic plants?

Yes, we have a special collection of exotic plants, including bonsai, orchids, air plants, and rare tropical species. Availability depends on the season.

Do you offer after-sales support or plant maintenance services?

Yes, we provide guidance on plant care even after purchase. For large-scale landscaping and corporate clients, we offer scheduled maintenance services to keep the plants healthy.
